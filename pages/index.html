Examination Instructions
Please read the following instructions carefully before applying for an examination.

  1. Login


    Login using your registered account to access the Student Dashboard.

  2. Enroll for an Examination


    Navigate to the Courses page and select the course you wish to take.

  3. Select Test Centre & Exam Date


    Choose your preferred examination centre and available exam date before submitting your application.

  4. Pending Approval


    After submitting the application, its status will change to Pending. Your application will be reviewed by the administrator.

  5. Application Approved


    If your application is approved, the status becomes Approved. Once the examination date has arrived, a Check button will be available.

  6. Mark as Attended


    Click the Check button after attending the examination. The status will be updated to Attended.

  7. Rejected Applications


    If your application is rejected, the status becomes Rejected. The administrator will provide a reason for the rejection.

  8. Editing an Application


    Applications can only be edited while they are in the Pending state.

  9. Reapply


    If your application has been rejected, you may correct the required details and submit a new application.
